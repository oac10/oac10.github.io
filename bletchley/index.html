How, When, Where, Who and What.

Read on to find out more detail about the codebreakers at Bletchley Park.

When?

During the Second world war, after the Germans had taken over most of Europe.

Where?

Bletchley Park, England.

Who?

Alan Turing and his team of Codebreakers cracked the code to the German Enigma machine.

What?

The Germans had been sinking US supply ships and the commands were given using the Enigma code. By secretly breaking the code the Allies would be able to know where the Germans would attack next.

How?

Turing used a computer called a "bombe". It was 2 metres tall and weighed a ton!

Additional information

The Codebreaking operation was incredibly secret. No one who worked there was allowed to say anything that might give away the secrets.
